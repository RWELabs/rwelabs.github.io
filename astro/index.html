      Introducing Astroneer Launcher

      to improve your game experience

      after having a game save go corrupt with no backup, we realised that the way Astroneer saves games is flawed. that's why we created the Astroneer Launcher.

      Never lose progress again

      automatic backups on launch

      every time you launch Astroneer with the Astroneer Launcher, you'll have the peace of mind knowing that a backup is being created automatically, saving it in a separate directory managed by the Astroneer Launcher

      You're in complete control

      backup management

      backup your saves manually with just a few clicks and have peace of mind knowing that you stay in control of your data. all data is stored locally and accessible through file explorer for complete control.

      Easy to install and set up

      Works with Steam

      With just a few clicks, you can make Astroneer Launcher the default program to open when you click "Play" from Steam, so you'll always benefit from Astroneer Launcher's features.
