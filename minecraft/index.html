      Introducing TBP Launcher

      a brand new Minecraft launcher for members of The Baked Potatarium that allows for easy content distribution and download, news, announcements and events publishing - with more possibilities available for the future.

      Stay up to date

      get the latest news and events

      from the main dashboard, users get curated news and announcements along with any server wide events that are taking place soon. filter through news, events or the curated tab to get the information that benefits you. with a single click, you can learn more and RSVP to events.

      Revisit the past

      download previous world saves

      The Baked Potatarium is a Minecraft server with a longstanding history, with year long "seasons" taking place in their own Minecraft world. players can look back on their previous achievements and revisit the past by downloading previous world saves with just a few clicks.

      One click content distribution

      easily download and install files required to play

      whether an in game event requires a halloween themed resource pack, or in the event that the server requires mods to be installed, users can easily download and install content with a single click. this is dynamic and scalable, and doesn't require the user to update the application to download newly available content.

      Use your existing account

      authenticate with your existing Discord account

      Discord is ingrained in the TBP community, being the method of whitelisting, moderation as well as voice and text chat between players. with all players requiring a Discord account and to be a member of the Discord server, we made it easy to sign in to the TBP Launcher using your existing Discord account.

      Minimum Specs

      Windows 7
      50mb Available Disk Space*
      a valid and legal Minecraft installation.

      Recommended Specs

      Windows 10 or 11
      350mb Available Disk Space*
      a valid and legal Minecraft installation

      Download for Windows

      *The minimum and recommended disk space requirements does not account for the Microsoft Edge WebView installation that may occur as a dependency of the application, the first time you run it. It also does not account for space to download additional content such as mods and resource packs.
